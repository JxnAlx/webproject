Some places around Fort Collins that I recomment that you try:

Asian Grill

I recomend this place because of its delicous asian cousine. There lunch special during lunch hours offers a choice of rice or noodles, and two meats. I definently recomend the lunch speical here.

Gib's Bagels

If you are looking for something light, I recomend gibs bagels. They have a huge selection of bagels and toppings. Definently recomend for the health conscious consumer.

Noodles

Noodles & Company has a huge selection of foreign dishes from all over the world. Their Macaroni & Cheese always keeps me coming back for more.
